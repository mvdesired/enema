These technologies are small files placed on your computer, tablet, mobile phone, or whatever device you are browsing with (collectively, "device") that allows us to record information when you visit or interact with our websites, applications, and other tools.

Cookies are small text files that are placed in the memory of your browser or device when you visit a website. They allow the website to recognize that particular browser or device. We use cookies in various ways, such as:

●Authenticating you on our website so that we can provide the services you requested.

●Keeping track of information you have provided us (for example, keeping items in your shopping cart).

●Remembering your website preferences.

●Measuring your use of our websites so that we can improve them and tailor them to your interests.

●Web beacons are snippets of programming code included in web pages, emails, and ads that notifies us or our trusted third party service providers when those web pages, emails or ads have been viewed or interacted with.

Cookies, web beacons and similar technologies serve various purposes, but are essential in allowing our websites and services to function properly. Without these technologies, we will not be able to provide you with essential features such as secure areas and shopping carts. Accepting the use of these technologies is a condition of using our websites, services, and other applications.
